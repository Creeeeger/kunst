Exhibitions (museums)


As a networker with access to many private collections, I often discuss with collectors why certain works should be made accessible to the public for a limited time. When this idea is followed through, both sides benefit: galleries and museums can enrich a special exhibition with a piece that would otherwise remain in private hands, and the owner can share the work with a wider audience while seeing it acknowledged anew in the art world.
