Your data

You are not obliged to provide personal data.
If you contact me via the form on this website, the details you enter are stored for six months to process your enquiry and handle any follow-up questions. This data is not passed on without your consent.
I do not process any "special category" personal data.

Data storage

Apart from transmitting the information you enter in the contact form, no additional data is stored when you get in touch via the website (for example, the IP address of your device). If a contract is concluded, only order-related data is stored outside the hosting environment (such as invoicing details).

The data you provide in the course of fulfilling an assignment is required to perform the contract or to take steps prior to entering into a contract. Invoicing data (and therefore customer data) is stored for seven years in accordance with statutory retention periods. General enquiries submitted via the contact form and personal data transmitted in this context without a contractual basis are stored for up to twelve months out of legitimate interest, for example to respond to follow-up questions.

Purposes of processing personal data

Processing and transferring data in the course of providing consulting or coaching services
Processing and transferring data to fulfil contractual obligations (for example, course participation)
Processing and transferring data for direct marketing

Processing is carried out on the basis of legal provisions, in particular the GDPR:
a) you have consented to the processing of your personal data until you revoke that consent;
b) the processing of your personal data is necessary to perform a contract or to carry out pre-contractual measures.

Right to information

You have the rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, data portability, revocation, and objection. You may request confirmation from the controller (usually Dagmar Härle) as to whether your personal data is being processed and which categories of data are involved. If this is the case, you are entitled to receive information about this personal data and the associated details.

Right of revocation

You have the right to object at any time to the processing of your personal data.

The controller will then no longer process the personal data unless compelling legitimate grounds for the processing can be demonstrated that override your interests, rights, and freedoms, or the processing serves to establish, exercise, or defend legal claims.

Personal data is not processed for direct marketing purposes.
